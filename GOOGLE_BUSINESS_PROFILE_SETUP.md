## 🔐 STEP 2: Verify Your Business

Google will ask you to verify ownership. Options:

### Option 1: Postcard (if you have physical address)
- Google mails a postcard with verification code
- Takes 5-14 days
- Enter code when received

### Option 2: Phone Verification
- Receive automated call or SMS
- Enter verification code
- Instant verification

### Option 3: Email Verification
- Receive email with verification link
- Click to verify
- Instant verification

### Option 4: Video Verification (for some businesses)
- Record a video tour of your business
- Upload to Google
- Approval within 3-5 days

**Recommended:** Use Phone or Email for fastest verification

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Business Not Showing in Search?
- Wait 2-4 weeks after verification
- Ensure business is verified
- Check if business is suspended
- Add more photos and information
- Get more reviews

### Can't Verify Business?
- Try different verification method
- Contact Google Business support
- Ensure business name matches exactly
- Check if business already exists

### Low Visibility?
- Add more photos (minimum 10)
- Get more reviews (minimum 10)
- Post updates regularly
- Respond to reviews
- Complete all profile sections

### Reviews Not Showing?
- Reviews take 24-48 hours to appear
- Ensure reviewers have Google accounts
- Reviews must follow Google's policies
- Don't incentivize reviews directly
